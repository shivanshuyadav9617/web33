# ArtLink Protocol

## Project Description

ArtLink Protocol is a revolutionary decentralized platform built on the Ethereum blockchain that bridges the gap between digital artists and art collectors worldwide. The protocol provides a comprehensive ecosystem for creating, trading, and collecting digital art as Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) with built-in royalty mechanisms that ensure artists continue to benefit from their work even after the initial sale. By eliminating intermediaries and leveraging smart contract automation, ArtLink Protocol empowers artists with full ownership and control over their creations while providing collectors with verifiable authenticity and complete provenance tracking. The platform implements advanced features including artist verification, automated royalty distribution, reputation systems, and transparent marketplace operations, creating a trustworthy environment for the digital art community.

## Project Vision

ArtLink Protocol envisions a future where digital artists have complete sovereignty over their creative works and receive fair compensation throughout the lifecycle of their art. Our mission is to democratize the digital art market by removing barriers to entry, eliminating exploitative intermediaries, and establishing a transparent, artist-first ecosystem built on blockchain technology. We aim to create a global community where creativity is rewarded, authenticity is guaranteed, and the value of digital art is permanently preserved through decentralized infrastructure. ArtLink Protocol seeks to become the premier destination for digital art creation and collection, fostering innovation in the creative economy while ensuring artists maintain perpetual rights to their work through automated royalty systems. We believe that by empowering artists with technology, we can unlock unprecedented creative potential and establish new standards for fair compensation in the digital age.

## Key Features

- Open registration system for all digital artists
- Optional verification process with badge system
- IPFS-based artist profile storage for decentralized identity
- Reputation tracking based on platform activity
- Verification fee of 0.05 ETH to ensure serious artists

### 2. **NFT Artwork Minting**
- One-click minting process for converting digital art to NFTs
- IPFS integration for decentralized artwork storage
- Customizable artwork metadata (title, description, pricing)
- Artist-defined royalty percentages (up to 30%)
- Automatic token ID generation and assignment
- Minimum price threshold to maintain marketplace quality

### 3. **Automated Royalty Distribution**
- Smart contract-enforced royalty payments on all secondary sales
- Artists earn passive income from resales automatically
- Transparent royalty calculation and distribution
- No manual intervention required
- Perpetual royalty rights for original creators
- Real-time earnings tracking

### 4. **Decentralized Marketplace**
- Peer-to-peer trading without intermediaries
- List and unlist artworks instantly
- Dynamic price updates for listed items
- Secure escrow system through smart contracts
- Automatic ownership transfer upon purchase
- Platform fee of only 2% (configurable)

### 5. **Comprehensive Provenance Tracking**
- Complete ownership history for every artwork
- Immutable record of all transactions
- Verifiable authenticity through blockchain
- Sales counter for each artwork
- Primary and secondary sale differentiation
- Transparent pricing history

### 6. **Reputation and Incentive System**
- Artists earn reputation points for platform activities
- Reputation increases with each artwork creation
- Bonus reputation for successful sales

- Gamification elements to encourage participation
- Long-term engagement rewards

### 7. **Secure Transaction Processing**
- Reentrancy protection on all value transfers
- Automatic refund for overpayment
- Multi-step payment distribution (seller, artist, platform)
- Failed transaction rollback mechanisms
- Gas-optimized operations
- Emergency withdrawal capabilities

### 8. **Artist and Collector Dashboards**
- View all artworks created by an artist

- Monitor earnings and sales statistics
- Access reputation scores and achievements

### 9. **Platform Analytics**
- Total artworks minted counter

### 10. **Flexible Marketplace Operations**
